pinecity mall

our history

Pine city mall was established in 1992, by Mzobe brothers. it has developed to become one of the top south african economic centres. the main goal is to ensure that customers get the best customer service there is to provide. that entails keeping our fleet of stores up to standard, customer-friendly, improved security inside the mall, spacious parking, bi-monthly customer competitions to reward our customers for spending their hard-earned cash.
